# UPOV Launches Global PVP Certificate

UPOV has launched a new PVP Certificate for private sector professionals in plant variety protection. The internationally recognized program validates expertise in the UPOV system, supports career advancement, and strengthens professional standing across seed and plant breeding sectors. Open globally, it offers a credible benchmark for knowledge of international PVP standards, best practices, and implementation quality.
