# Biotech Just Hit Fast Forward. Is Europe’s Seed Sector Ready?

AI, CRISPR, genomic prediction and speed breeding are transforming plant breeding at unprecedented speed. Wageningen University’s Richard Harrison says Europe’s seed sector faces a defining moment as New Genomic Techniques reshape regulation, investment and innovation access. Faster breeding tools, AI-driven research and accessible gene editing could diversify crops, strengthen resilience and accelerate sustainable agriculture while raising critical questions about patents, biodiversity and Europe’s future food systems.
