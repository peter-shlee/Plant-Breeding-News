# New Jalapeño, Murupi and Ornamental Peppers Move to On-Farm Testing in Brazil

New jalapeño, murupi, fresh-market, and ornamental pepper materials from Embrapa Vegetables are advancing to on-farm testing in Brazil. Developed to meet demands from growers, processors, and ornamental markets, the cultivars aim to improve agronomic, industrial, and functional traits. The program supports sustainable production, organic systems, mechanized harvesting, and greater competitiveness across Brazil’s expanding pepper value chain.
