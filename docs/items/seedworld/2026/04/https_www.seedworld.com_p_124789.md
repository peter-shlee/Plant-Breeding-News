# Breeders Trust Settles Case Over Illegal Seed Potato Listing In Denmark

Danish potato grower settles Breeders Trust case after a Facebook listing offered protected Solist seed potatoes, underscoring that online advertisements can infringe plant breeders’ rights even without a sale. The case highlights strict seed potato certification rules, legal and phytosanitary risks, and the growing responsibility of online platforms to prevent unlawful potato trading.
