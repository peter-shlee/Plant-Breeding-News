# New Soybean Variety Targets Herbicide-Resistant Weeds

Embrapa Soja and Caramuru Alimentos have launched BRS 579, a high-yield soybean cultivar for north-central Mato Grosso that combines strong plant health with STS herbicide tolerance. The new variety offers growers greater flexibility in weed management, resistance to key nematodes, and an alternative to glyphosate-based systems, supporting productivity, profitability, and more sustainable soybean production in Brazil.
