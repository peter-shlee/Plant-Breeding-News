# Dirty Ships, Lifted Embargoes and the Hidden Power of Grain

This article examines how climate-driven famine and uninspected grain trade helped spread the Black Death, drawing parallels to today’s risks from illegal and counterfeit seeds. It highlights how uncertified seed trade bypasses biosecurity, inspection and traceability, threatening agriculture, farmers and the environment. Strong seed certification and enforcement are essential to prevent pests, diseases and economic losses.
