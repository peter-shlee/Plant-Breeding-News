# New Review Advances Precision Gene Insertion in Corn

A new review highlights advances in precision gene insertion in corn, focusing on site-specific transgene integration and genomic “safe harbors.” Led by GCCRC researchers, the study explains how next-generation genome editing tools can reduce randomness, improve gene stability, and lower development costs. The approach supports faster creation of drought-tolerant, climate-resilient corn varieties with greater regulatory predictability and efficiency.
