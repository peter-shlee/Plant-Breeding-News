# From Waste to Yield: Biochar Innovation Supports Crop Growth

Researchers developed an engineered sewage sludge biochar paired with Bacillus velezensis that boosts cabbage growth by improving nitrogen uptake and soil microbial activity. The biofertilizer increased plant biomass by up to 40%, while enhancing root colonization and soil health. This sustainable approach transforms waste into a valuable agricultural input, reducing dependence on chemical fertilizers and supporting greener crop production.
