# CPVR Evaluation: A Key Moment for the Future of Plant Variety Protection in Europe

The European Commission’s review of Community Plant Variety Rights (CPVR) legislation offers a key opportunity to strengthen EU plant breeding. Euroseeds says the current system provides reliable, cost-effective protection for new plant varieties, but challenges remain around farm-saved seed, enforcement, provisional protection, EDVs, and breeders’ rights. The evaluation could modernize plant variety protection and support innovation across Europe.
