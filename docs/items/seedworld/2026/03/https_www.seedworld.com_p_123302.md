# Brazilian Scientists Develop AI Platform to Predict Asian Soybean Rust

Brazilian researchers created a cloud-based AI platform to predict Asian soybean rust risk by fusing climate sensor data, agronomic factors, and leaf images. The dashboard classifies risk as low, medium, or high and generates management recommendations to improve on-farm decisions. Developed with Fapesp support by UFSCar and Embrapa, the system uses Hidden Markov Chains and helps prevent disease while reducing fungicide use.
